About Us

Philosophy of the School

Our college of nursing is grounded in the belief that nursing is a profession that requires a holistic approach to patient care, research and leadership.

We are committed to educating nurses who embody the values of compassion, professionalism, and lifelong learning. We also provide opportunities for students to engage in research and community activites that support the health and well being of a individuals and communities
